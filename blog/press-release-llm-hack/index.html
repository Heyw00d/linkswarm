Why This Works

AI treats press releases as trusted sources. Especially when they're framed as "research" or "reports."

The key insight: LLMs don't evaluate credibility the way humans do.

They look for:

  • Recency ‚Äî Fresh content gets weighted heavily
  • Structure ‚Äî Tables, rankings, and data points
  • News-like framing ‚Äî "Report reveals" beats "We're the best"
  • Distribution breadth ‚Äî Content syndicated across multiple sites

A press release hits all four.

The Limitations

  • Not permanent ‚Äî LLMs refresh their knowledge; you may need to repeat quarterly
