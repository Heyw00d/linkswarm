1. Introduction

LinkSwarm ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)

6. Data Retention

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.
